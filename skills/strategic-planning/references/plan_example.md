# Example Strategic Plan: New Mobile App Launch

## Goals & Vision
- **Goal:** Capture 10% market share in the fitness app segment within 12 months.
- **Vision:** Empower users to achieve daily activity targets through AI‑driven coaching.

## Objectives & Key Results (OKRs)

### Objective 1: Product Development
- KR 1.1: MVP released by **2026‑04‑01**.
- KR 1.2: 5 core features implemented (tracking, coaching, community, analytics, premium).
- KR 1.3: User onboarding completion rate > 85%.

### Objective 2: Market Adoption
- KR 2.1: 100k downloads within 6 months.
- KR 2.2: 30% of users convert to premium within 3 months.
- KR 2.3: Average daily active users (DAU) > 20k by month 9.

## Milestones & Timeline
| Milestone | Target Date | Owner |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Concept validation | 2026‑02‑15 | Product Lead |
| Design finalization | 2026‑03‑01 | UX Designer |
| MVP development start | 2026‑03‑05 | Engineering Lead |
| Beta release | 2026‑04‑15 | QA Lead |
| Public launch | 2026‑05‑01 | PM |
| Marketing campaign kickoff | 2026‑04‑20 | Marketing |

## Responsibilities
- **Product Lead:** Define roadmap, align stakeholders.
- **Engineering Lead:** Oversee development, ensure technical feasibility.
- **UX Designer:** Create user flows, UI mockups.
- **Marketing Lead:** Plan acquisition channels, budget.
- **Data Analyst:** Set up KPI dashboards.

## Risks & Mitigations
- **Risk:** Delayed feature integration.
  - *Mitigation:* Adopt agile sprints, weekly stand‑ups.
- **Risk:** Low user acquisition.
  - *Mitigation:* Pre‑launch beta incentives, partnership with fitness influencers.

## KPI Tracking
- **Monthly Active Users (MAU)** – tracked via analytics dashboard.
- **Retention Rate** – 30‑day cohort analysis.
- **Revenue** – Premium subscription revenue.
